Commissions

We offer commission services for those who want custom voicebanks created for different platforms.

Prices

Terms of Service

  • In this transaction, I, the service provider, will create a configuration file to make a set of specified voice samples compatible with the desired software.
  • Once your voicebank configuration is completed, I will create a small demo as proof of completion. From this point, you will receive your file once payment has been completed.
  • Unless otherwise agreed upon, I will require the client to credit me as the creator of the configuration, either within the contents of the voicebank itself, the voicebank's distribution source, or other publicly available venue associated with the voicebank.
  • YOU MAY NOT take credit for the creation of the completed configuration file.

Payments and Refunds

  • I only accept PayPal payments.
  • I will only send the finished work once I receive the payment.
  • You are entitled to up to 3 revisions within the first 36 hours of receiving your configured voicebank.
  • Please let me know when the bank has been downloaded, as it will not be stored for more than a week.
  • In case of dissatisfaction with the received product, a 50% refund will be granted only if the client requests it before subsequent revisions are completed.

Work Speed

  • I'll endeavor to complete your commission as soon as possible, but I ask for your patience.
  • The estimated time to complete a commission is specified in the price table for each software; however, the time may vary depending on my workload and the commission's complexity. You will be informed in case of any delays or cancellations.
  • I typically focus on one commission at a time. Please take note.

Notes

  • The service provider reserves the right to deny any request.
  • You must provide the entire voicebank recordings for me to download.
  • In the event of incorrect or broken recordings, I will compile a list of samples needed to complete the bank and request their re-recording.
  • I will not clean up audio files.
